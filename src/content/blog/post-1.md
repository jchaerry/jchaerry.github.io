## 👋 Hello World!

Hello! I'm **chaeri Jung**, an undergraduate majoring in Artificial Intelligence and Big Data at Sookmyung Women's University, currently working as an undergraduate research intern at SNSec Lab. 

I finally decided to launch my personal website and blog to gather all my study notes and project records that were previously scattered across Notion and GitHub READMEs into one organized space!

## 🛠️ How was this site built?

This portfolio website is built with **Astro.js** and **Tailwind CSS**. 
Stepping out of my usual setup, I configured a local development environment using **WSL2 and Docker**. Analyzing the template code, stripping out unnecessary parts, fixing errors, and customizing it to fit my needs turned out to be a really fun mini-project in itself.

## 🚀 What kind of content will be posted here?

I am deeply interested in **Security** technologies based on **Deep Learning** and **Self-Supervised Learning**. Moving forward, I plan to fill this blog with the following topics:

* **Security Research:** Paper reviews and research logs focused on Anomaly Detection and Intrusion Detection.
* **Future Interests:** Explorations into Adversarial Machine Learning and Concept Drift Adaptation.
* **Project Log:** Progress updates and troubleshooting from various data-driven projects I am working on at my research lab.
* **Dev Log:** Minor struggles and solutions from coding and environment setups, much like the process of building this very website.

## Wrapping up

Beyond simply accumulating knowledge, my goal is to consistently grow and become someone who solves real-world problems through data-driven approaches.
